The Midlands African Choir took to the open-air stage at Cannon Hill Park's World Music Festival in July 2024, delivering a sun-drenched performance that captivated an audience of over 1,200 festival-goers. The set drew from a rich repertoire of highlife, Afrobeats-influenced choral arrangements, and traditional call-and-response songs from across the African continent, with the choir's vibrant energy and tight harmonies earning a standing ovation and an enthusiastic encore from the crowd.
