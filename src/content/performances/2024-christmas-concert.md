The Midlands African Choir's Christmas Concert 2024 was a truly memorable evening at Birmingham Town Hall, bringing together over 400 audience members for a programme that wove together traditional African harmonies, gospel anthems, and beloved Christmas carols. The choir performed a specially commissioned arrangement of "Silent Night" in three West African languages alongside rousing renditions of Ghanaian and Nigerian festive songs, creating a warm and inclusive celebration that reflected the rich cultural tapestry of the Midlands community.
